# Automate phone calls with AI

Welcome to the Autocalls.ai documentation hub. Whether you're a product owner, marketer, or developer, these guides will help you build, launch, and scale AI-powered voice experiences.

<Note>
  Need help getting started? Jump to our <a href="/getting-started/core-concepts">core concepts guide</a> or <a href="https://cloudone-ai.com/contact">contact support</a>.
</Note>

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up on Autocalls.ai">Create your free account and access your dashboard.</Step>
  <Step title="Create your first AI assistant">Define the prompt, choose a voice, and connect any tools you need.</Step>
  <Step title="Start making & receiving calls">Attach a phone number, launch a campaign, or route inbound calls to your assistant.</Step>
</Steps>
